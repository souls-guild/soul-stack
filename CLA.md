# Soul Stack Contributor License Agreement

Thank you for contributing to Soul Stack. This Contributor License Agreement ("CLA")
sets out the terms under which you contribute. It is a **license-back** agreement:
**you keep the copyright to your contribution** and grant the project a license to use
and relicense it. You do **not** assign or transfer ownership.

> **Why this exists.** Soul Stack's core is released under the
> [Business Source License 1.1](LICENSE) (fair-code) with an Additional Use Grant and
> a Change License (Apache 2.0). To offer those terms — and to make future license
> changes — on behalf of everyone, the project needs a license to every contribution
> it ships. Without a CLA in place before the first external contribution, a later
> license change would require tracking down and re-obtaining permission from every
> past contributor. This agreement is modeled on the license-grant approach used by
> the Apache Software Foundation and by other fair-code projects (n8n, Sentry,
> CockroachDB).

You accept this CLA when you sign it on your first pull request (see
[Signing](#signing) below).

## Signing

Signing is automated. When you open your first pull request, a bot comments asking you
to sign; you sign by replying to that comment with the exact sentence it gives you.
Your signature (your GitHub username and the PR reference) is stored **in this
repository** — there is no third-party service. Subsequent PRs are recognized
automatically.

If you contribute on behalf of a company and need a company-wide agreement instead of
individual signatures, contact the maintainers via [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
